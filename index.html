  • 100% dependent on offline channels — no digital pipeline whatsoever
  • Could not reach pan-India buyers beyond their local geography
  • No brand awareness outside their immediate network
  • Initially getting irrelevant, low-intent leads after going online (trash leads)
🧠 My Strategy

I built a complete B2B lead generation system from scratch using Meta Ads with a WhatsApp Direct Inquiry funnel — targeting decision-makers in the wood & furniture supply chain.

01
Audience Research & Targeting Setup
Identified and targeted plywood manufacturers, furniture businesses, dealers & wholesalers using detailed interest + behaviour targeting on Meta.
02
Video Creative Production

03

🔴 The Challenge

The client was an E-commerce / Retail brand struggling to generate consistent online sales from paid traffic. They were competing in a crowded search landscape with high keyword costs and poor ROI from existing campaigns.

  • Low online visibility in a highly competitive market
  • Existing campaigns wasting budget on irrelevant, low-intent clicks
  • High competition on core search keywords driving CPC up
  • No structured Shopping or Remarketing campaigns in place
  • No scalable growth system — results were inconsistent month to month
🧠 My Strategy

I built a full-funnel Google Ads growth system using 4 campaign types working together — Shopping, Search, Smart Shopping, and Remarketing — each targeting a different stage of the buyer journey.

01
Campaign Architecture by Product Category
Structured all campaigns by product categories instead of one broad campaign — this gave granular control over bidding, budget allocation, and performance tracking per category.
02
Shopping Feed Optimization
Optimised the product feed for Google Shopping — improved titles, descriptions, and attributes to increase product visibility and relevance score, directly improving CTR and conversion rate.
03
Keyword Strategy — High Intent Focus
Targeted high-intent, purchase-ready keywords. Aggressively added negative keywords to eliminate wasteful spend from irrelevant searches — this alone significantly reduced cost per conversion.
04
Bid Optimisation + Remarketing
Applied bid adjustments for best-performing campaigns, devices, and audience segments. Added remarketing layers to re-engage past visitors and cart abandoners — maximising conversion value from existing traffic.
05
Continuous A/B Testing & Scaling
Ran continuous A/B tests on ad copies and creatives. Scaled winning campaigns with budget reallocation from underperformers. Built a repeatable system for sustainable month-on-month growth.
⚡ Key Achievement

Average CPC of just ₹0.88 across 81,582 clicks in a competitive e-commerce market — this is an exceptionally low cost per click that reflects precise targeting, strong quality scores, and well-optimised product feeds.

1,010 conversions from ₹71,588 spend — a cost per conversion of ₹70.88, delivering a scalable, profitable paid growth system that the client could confidently increase budget on.

🔴 The Challenge

A Real Estate client in Noida was running campaigns in one of India's most competitive property markets. Their biggest problem wasn't getting leads — it was getting the right leads. Low-quality inquiries were wasting the sales team's time and budget was going to people who had zero intent to buy.

  • Highly competitive Noida real estate market with high CPL
  • Low-quality leads flooding the pipeline — wasting sales team hours
  • Need for serious property buyers and investors, not casual inquiries
  • Objective: Generate actual site visits and serious buyer conversations
🧠 My Strategy

I designed a hyper-local Meta Ads campaign focused exclusively on high-income audiences and property investors in Noida and nearby areas — with a multi-touchpoint funnel that moved buyers from ad to site visit.

01
Hyper-Local Targeting — Noida & Nearby Areas
Focused targeting on Noida and surrounding high-income localities. Audience profile: high-income individuals, property investors, and people showing real estate purchase intent — eliminating broad, irrelevant audiences entirely.
02
Premium Creative — Farmhouse Lifestyle Showcase
Designed premium creatives showcasing farmhouse lifestyle and location benefits — aspirational visuals that attracted serious buyers rather than casual browsers. Creative was built to pre-qualify interest before the click.
03
Multi-Touchpoint Funnel: Ads → Calls + WhatsApp → Site Visit
Used Lead Form ads + Call campaigns simultaneously to capture different buyer types. Added retargeting for warm audiences who engaged but didn't convert — maximising every rupee of spend.
04
Sales Team Alignment
Aligned ad campaigns directly with sales team follow-up process — ensuring leads were contacted within minutes of inquiry. This alignment between marketing and sales drove faster closures and improved conversion rate from lead to site visit.
⚡ Key Achievement

CPL maintained between ₹31–₹148 across multiple campaigns in a highly competitive real estate market — with the best campaign delivering leads at just ₹31 per lead.

100+ qualified leads from only ₹4,377 spend — an extremely efficient budget utilisation that proved hyper-local targeting + premium creatives outperform broad campaigns in real estate.
